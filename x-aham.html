Overshadowed Hamilton (Glitch Art)


Overshadowed Hamilton is glitch art made during my Art-74 class and it was created by taking the source code of Alexander Hamilton's portrait and pasting the lyrics of the song Hamilton from the broadway musical Hamilton randomly throughout the code's file. It's not meant to be visually appealing, but the content in the code generated this interesting ghost-like outline of Alexander Hamilton as if he was trying to telling me something. I believe this glitch ironically represents how the actual Alexander Hamilton is now being overshadowed by the musical about him.

Humor me and think about it. What pops up in your head first when you think of Hamilton: The person or the musical?
