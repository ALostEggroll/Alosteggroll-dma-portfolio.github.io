Sloth (Digital Art)


This project was made during my Art-3 class and created using photoshop. Sloth is one of the 7 deadly sins. Inspired by the Obama HOPE poster, I mashed it up with a sloth to criticize congress's slow nature to pass laws and changes in our government. In context of the 7 deadly sins, it is ironic to think that the USA, the nation under god, is hippocratic with their founding father's beliefs.
