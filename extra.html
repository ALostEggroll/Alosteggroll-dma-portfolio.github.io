Lucid Dream (artist/co-story writer)

Lucid dream is a video game made using twine by Cole and Christian (me) exploring human relations and our feelings towards them. This project was also diplayed during the CADRE Mentorship's Dreamscape exhibition in new art city!

Link to the game: Lucid Dream


Dog-N-Found (artist/animator)

Dog-n-Found was a story about a VERY LONG lost dog trying to find it's owner by helping out others who would help in return. This game was made by Adrian, Nathan, Ryan, Gokul, and Christian (me).
