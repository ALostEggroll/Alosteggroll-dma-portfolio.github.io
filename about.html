Artist Statement

Pleased to meet you and thank you for visiting my portfoliio!

I'm Christian Acosta, a friendly filpino immigrant raised in San Jose California. I come from a traditional animation background and I love animating my work using maya, after effects, and photoshop. Most of my art work consists of comedy, animations, video game projects, and bringing awareness to social equality and climate change. I've seen first hand how stress can make people sad or angry, so I believe I can help others enjoy life by laughing at the humor in some of my works. On the other hand, I also believe some issues can't be solved by laughing it off. So I adapt my work depending on the current mood of the world. If we're too angry I'll make you laugh, but if we're too content I'll make you aware. I often like to explore concepts
