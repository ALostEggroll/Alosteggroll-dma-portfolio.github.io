3D Ball with Tail animation + Character vault



This was my 3D animated showcase I made during my time at De Anza and created using maya. Here I displayed the blocking process of animating a ball with a tail and the final rendered product with an extra full body character vault using an IK and FK rig. The obstacle course was inspired by the Mario platformer games.
