3D Ball with Tail animation + Character Vault (3D Animation)



This was my 3D animated showcase I made during my time at De Anza and created using maya. Here I displayed the blocking process of animating a ball with a tail and the final rendered product with an extra full body character vault using an IK and FK rig. The obstacle course was inspired by the Mario and Sonic platformer games. This was my first time animating in maya and it felt very natural because of my traditional animation background. This project makes me want to do more 3D animated works and improve my 3D character animations.
