2020 Animated


I believed 2020 was going to be the best year. BOY WAS I WRONG! In a short animated summary 2020 was met with wildfires, Covid-19, police brutality, and the 2020 election. In this animated summary, each asset is animated with respects to the subject matter. The fire spreads to engulf the first 2; the 0 for covid starts small then grows in both the similar ways it was regarded as a literally and figuratively small issue; the second 2 representing the police brutality came in like a flash and the smoke represents the tier gas used during the protests; finally the election drops in affecting the previous events and the outcome caps off the end of 2020.
